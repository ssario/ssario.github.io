Dr. Samet Sarıoğlan

Hi, I’m Samet!

I received my PhD degree from Hacettepe University Department of Mathematics in 2019. Most of my research focus on algebraic spline theory.

During Summer Semester of 2024, I am a part-time lecturer at Başkent University. I was a lecturer at TED University, Hacettepe University and Turkish Aeronautical Association University in previous semesters.
